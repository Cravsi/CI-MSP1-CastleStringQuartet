The String Quartet For Every Event

This fabulous quartet of professional musicians have provided performances for clients such as Goldman Sachs, IRFU, Dáil Éireann, Brown Thomas and Brian O'Driscoll.

Founded at the Royal Irish Academy of Music, these dynamic musicians have since become a staggering success. Offering an extensive range of pieces from Hans Zimmer to Bach, James Bond to Pirates of the Carribean and Taylor Swift to Queen. You're sure to be amazed at their musical versatility.

If you're looking to hire a string ensemble with flair and a modern style then you've come to the right place. Castle Strings are happy to put together bespoke arrangements to suit your special day so just let us know what special song you have in mind.

About The Players!

Katie O'Connor

Beginning young and rising to national recognition by age eight, Irish violinist Katie O'connor is one of the more decorated violinists of recent times.
She was the leader of the National Children’s Orchestra of Great Britain and went on to study at the Yehudi Menuhin School in England.

Jane Hyde

Born in Sligo, Jane began violin at age four, then appeared on the world stage by age twelve, shocking audiences with her talent
This turned into a lifelong career of performing around the world, especially in Britain, and recording a great many songs with other famous musicians.

Aoife Wall

Norwegian violinist Aoife began her musical career at age six and rose to significant talent in the years after with her beautiful renditions of many popular classics.
She keeps her performances simple and doesn’t often play with a full orchestra.

Laura O'Shea

Laura's family background in music made it simple for her to learn violin from a young age.
She also knows her way around the piano, her mother viewing piano as a skill for any aspiring musician to know.
O'Shea has performed all over the world with various different orchestras.
